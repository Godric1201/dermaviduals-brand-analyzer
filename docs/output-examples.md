# Output Examples

This document summarizes the main outputs generated by the AI Brand Visibility / GEO Audit Tool.

## Visibility Scorecard

The visibility scorecard summarizes how often the target brand appears in AI-generated recommendation answers.

Typical metrics include:

- Total brand mentions
- Prompts visible
- Average visibility score
- First appearance position
- Estimated rank
- Share of Voice

## Competitor Benchmark

The competitor benchmark compares the target brand against tracked competitors.

It helps answer:

- Which brands appear most often?
- Which competitors dominate AI recommendations?
- Which brands are associated with stronger trust signals?
- Where does the target brand underperform?

## Share of Voice

Share of Voice estimates how much of the AI recommendation space is captured by each tracked brand.

This is useful for comparing relative visibility across competitors.

## Brand Intelligence Findings

The Brand Intelligence module extracts qualitative positioning signals from AI-generated responses, such as:

- Recommendation drivers
- Competitor-owned associations
- Market signals
- Positioning gaps
- Trust signals
- Content opportunities

## GEO Content Roadmap

The GEO Content Roadmap translates visibility gaps into content recommendations.

Typical roadmap fields include:

- Query intent
- Target association
- Recommended content action
- Evidence needed
- Expected benchmark impact
- Priority level

## Executive Reports

The tool can export reports in:

- Markdown
- DOCX

A typical report includes:

- Executive Summary
- Competitive Benchmark
- Trigger-Level Visibility Findings
- Strategic Priorities
- GEO Content Roadmap
- Measurement Plan
- Brand Intelligence Appendix
- AI Visibility Strategy Deep Dive

## Benchmark Snapshots

Benchmark snapshots are exported as JSON files and can be compared over time.

They are useful for tracking whether content and positioning improvements lead to better AI visibility.
